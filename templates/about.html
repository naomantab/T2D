About T2D SNP Atlas

Learn more about this website and the team

Welcome to the T2D SNP Atlas! This platform is designed to help you explore and analyze single nucleotide polymorphisms (SNPs) associated with Type 2 Diabetes (T2D). Our database provides comprehensive genomic data across multiple populations and this website can allow you to seamlessly navigate through to find your SNP of interest. Use the navigation links above to search for specific SNPs, explore population data, or download reports for further analysis.

About the team:

This project was created by the Jammy Jellyfish team as part of the Group Project Module in MSc Bioinformatics at Queen Mary University of London.
